Features

Everything your school needs in one place

From admission to alumni — EMS covers the full student lifecycle with zero data silos.

🎓
Student Management
Maintain complete student profiles — admission, personal details, documents, academic history, and parent contacts — in one searchable database.
📋
Attendance Tracking
Mark daily attendance per class with one tap. Automated SMS and push notifications alert parents when their child is absent.
💰
Fee Management
Flexible fee heads, concession management, online payments, auto-receipts, and overdue reminders — your accountant will thank you.
📝
Exams & Results
Schedule exams, enter marks, compute grades, and publish results. Supports CBSE, ICSE, and State Board report card formats.
👩‍🏫
Staff & Payroll
Manage staff profiles, timetables, leave requests, and payroll — all in sync. Digital payslips delivered automatically.
📱
Parent App
Parents get a dedicated mobile app for attendance, results, fee dues, remarks, homework, and real-time chat with teachers.
🗓️
Timetable Builder
Drag-and-drop timetable creation with automatic conflict detection. Substitution management included.
📚
Homework Management
Teachers assign, track, and grade homework digitally. Students and parents receive assignment notifications instantly.
🔔
Communication Suite
SMS, email, WhatsApp, and push notifications for announcements, reminders, and individual messaging — all from one inbox.
🚌
Transport Management
Track bus routes, assign students to vehicles, and monitor transport fees with the built-in transport module.
📊
Analytics & Reports
Real-time dashboards for attendance trends, fee collection, academic performance, and staff utilisation. Export any report to Excel or PDF.
🔒
Multi-Tenant Security
Every school's data is fully isolated. Role-based access ensures staff see only what they need. GDPR and FERPA compliant.
